Matthew Martinez

Buisnessman and Fashion Designer


Matthew Martinez, who was born on October 2, 2005, is a Guatemalan-Mexican American businessman, fashion designer and former lawyer. He is known for being the founder of the skateboarding shop and clothing brand Korupt.
Matthew Martinez was born on October 2, 2005, in the state of California and city of Los Angeles. His Mexican father was formely a barber, and his Guatemalan mother is a former teacher. His parents divorced when he was around 3 years old. At one years old, Martinez's parents relocated from Washington D.C. to California.In 2024 Martinez moved out of L.A when he was 19 years old, and settled in Staten Island, New York City. In 2024 Martinez got his first job at a local skate shop, it is then when he gathered inspiration from the culture and surroundings to try and start something of his own

In 2026 Matthew Martinez finally gained the courage and money to be able to open and kick start his brand, which was first going to be called Shrugs, but ended up going with Korupt. He opened the first store in Manhattan and only sold T-shirts and decks, but as of now there are 20 worldwide locations and sells a variety of items. He has also been very succesful and collaborated with big name brands like Louis Vutton, Supreme, Stussy, and Rick Owens. His brand is everywhere and celebrities are almost always wearing a piece from Korupt.
